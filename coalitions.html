Labour

New Zealand Labour Party

Labour holds values such as well paying jobs, reliable healthcare, and affordable housing, aligning strongly with our movement's priorities. We chose to form a partnership with the Labour Party as merging with a larger party that supports our views will greatly increase our chances of success.

Green

New Zealand Green Party

We chose to partner with the Green Party as our views align with theirs, such as supporting the environment, more affordable living, and generally an easier life for the average New Zealander.

Māori Party

Te Pāti Māori

The Māori Party (Te Pāti Māori) supports equal representation and value of the people and minorities, especially the Māori community. We chose to join with the Māori Party as they generally prioritise a people-first view, aligning with ours.
